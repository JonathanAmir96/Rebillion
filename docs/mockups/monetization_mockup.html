Rebillion — Live-Ops & Monetization Surfaces Mock-up

The four surfaces where money and live-ops touch the game, drawn together so the boundaries between them stay visible: the Wayfarer's Charter (shards-bought, never real money — BATTLE_PASS.md), the Cogwork Capsule (the single real-money product, bounded by amendment PA-001 — GACHAPON.md), the appearance layer both feed (COSMETICS.md), and in-world sponsor billboards (MONETIZATION.md §3.2). Sprites/hex values are illustrative placeholders — art identity is owned by ART_BIBLE.yaml. Gold numbered pins are design annotations, not game UI.

▶ The charter panel completes a daily stamp; the capsule runs a full pull. Both loop. Motion is honored per prefers-reduced-motion. Nothing on this page exists in the interim solo build's store — the solo build ships no store at all (GACHAPON §6, MONETIZATION §5).

2 · The Cogwork Capsule (the game's single real-money product, PA-001)

1 One ticket → one server-side roll. The client never rolls a prize GACHAPON §4/§7, PERSISTENCE §7
2 40-pull cosmetic pity, per character, reset on any cosmetic hit GACHAPON §5
3 Odds are displayed before every pull — structural, so the compliance obligation cannot be designed away GACHAPON §1.4/§8
4 10 tickets / character / week, hard. No "limited-time" pressure mechanics, ever GACHAPON §1.3
power ceiling Equipment rolls clamp at rarity rare. Never dispensed: epic, legendary, boss uniques, or any capsule-only stat item.
earnable free Tickets flow from both charter lanes and a rare world drop. Paying accelerates pulls, never unlocks unreachable content.
no money → shards bridge Tickets are never bought or sold for shards, and every dispensed prize vendors for 0.
regionally disableable Where paid loot boxes are restricted, the SKU switches off and the machine falls back to earned-tickets-only — which the solo build already is.
changing any cap is a new pillar amendment, not a Phase D tune. PA-001's bounds are load-bearing, not defaults.

Capsule exclusives are allowed — for cosmetics only. PA-001's power-parity rules govern stats, not looks: a capsule cosmetic is a COSMETICS.md unlock entry with zero stats, character-bound, no vendor or trade value, occupying no equipment slot. A duplicate cosmetic re-rolls once against the capsule list; a full-collection duplicate falls back to an emberstone cache.

4 · In-world sponsor billboards (MONETIZATION.md §3.2)

Rejected outright, and recorded as rejected: pay-to-win of any kind · programmatic in-game advertising · subscriptions that gate content or power · paid convenience (repair fees, energy systems, revive tokens) · durability introduced as a monetization hook. gleam, the proposed premium currency, is Provisional in the GLOSSARY, spends only in the cosmetic/supporter store, is account-bound, and never converts to shards.
